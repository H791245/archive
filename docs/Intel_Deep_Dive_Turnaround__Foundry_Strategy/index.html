Key Risks & Market Implications
1. Yield Stall Risk (Highest Impact)
If Intel stalls at 40-50% yields in Q2 2026 instead of targeting 65-75%, customers will bail. Foundry business collapses before it starts. Timeline: Make-or-break period is Feb-Mar 2026.
2. Government Stake Complications
U.S. ownership (10% stake, Aug 2025) creates geopolitical concerns. European/Japanese/Korean customers worry about U.S. control. TSMC's Arizona fabs now look like safer hedge. Cost: Likely 15-20% of potential Asian customers in 2026-2027.
3. Customer Concentration Risk
Only Intel is confirmed. If NVIDIA/Apple/Qualcomm don't materialize by mid-2026, foundry narrative breaks. Stock reprices to reflect "Intel is still just an IDM."
4. Cost Reduction Execution
Intel needs $8-10B cost savings by 2025 exit + 30-40% per-wafer cost reduction. Missing these targets makes foundry margins uncompetitive vs TSMC's 50%+.
