The Crisis Points

🔒

Sovereignty Concerns

High Impact

US demands exclusive control over investment project selection. Japan insisted on maintaining sovereign control through its own development bank.

💱

Currency Risk

High Impact

Massive capital outflow could impact yen and foreign reserves. Japan's finance ministry refused to transfer funds without control guarantees.

💰

Profit Distribution

High Impact

US gets 90% of profits after Japan recoups principal. This lopsided arrangement sparked outrage in Tokyo's political circles.

😠

Public Opinion

Critical Impact

Japanese public saw deal as capitulation. PM Ishiba's approval rating collapsed below 20%, leading to his resignation.

📄

Legal Status

Medium Impact

Framework not a formal treaty, open to interpretation. This legal ambiguity allowed both sides to dispute terms.
