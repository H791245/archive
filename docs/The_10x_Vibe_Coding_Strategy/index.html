Phases 1 & 2: The 4 Core Actions

The first two phases are built on four discrete actions. The setup (Steps 1-2) is done once, while the workflow (Steps 3-4) is your active coding loop.

Step 1: Perfect Context

Action: Add your project's core context to the top of your `claude.md` file.

Why: Every new session starts with perfect context automatically. No more copy-pasting setup prompts.

Phase 3: Session Health & Maintenance

This is the most critical phase for maintaining long-term quality. How you manage your context window determines the AI's effectiveness. The goal is to be proactive, not reactive.

Step 5: Be Proactive

Proactive Action: Use the `clear` command *before* you hit the context limit.

Why: It's a fresh start. It dumps legacy junk, saves tokens, and resets context, ensuring 100% quality.

Reactive "Compact": If the session auto-summarizes, be careful. The quality starts to degrade after 1-2 compacts.

Context Strategy Comparison

A "Proactive Clear" is always cheaper and higher quality than letting the session "Compact" and degrade.

The "Compact" Trap: Quality Degradation

The "compact" command summarizes history, but details are lost. After 1-2 compacts, the AI's response quality drops off sharply.
