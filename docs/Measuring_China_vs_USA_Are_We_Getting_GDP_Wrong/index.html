Imputation Problem

$4 trillion (14% of US GDP)

The US adds estimated values for non-cash economic activity that never actually happens.

Examples:
  • Imputed rent (if you owned your home)
  • Employer-provided health insurance value
China & most countries: Don't include these imputations. If there are no actual cash flows, it doesn't get counted.

üí° Key Takeaways
