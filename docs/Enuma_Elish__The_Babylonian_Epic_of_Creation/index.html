When on High:
The Babylonian Epic of Creation
Enuma Elish is the ancient Babylonian epic of creation, composed ~1400 BCE and discovered in 1849, inscribed upon seven clay tablets in cuneiform. This cosmic saga tells the story of the universe's birth, the gods' struggle for power, and the triumph of order over chaos.
