The Chip Battle: Semiconductors as Strategic Weapons

US Actions

  • Export controls since 2022 blocking advanced chips to China
  • CHIPS Act: $52.7 billion for US semiconductor ecosystem
  • TSMC building fabs in Arizona

China Response

  • $47.5 billion investment in 2024
  • Goal: 70% semiconductor self-sufficiency by 2030
  • SMIC produced 7nm chips with older DUV tools (innovation under constraint)
  • Huawei Ascend 910C AI chips competing despite sanctions
