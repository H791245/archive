‚ö†Ô∏è Key Risks & Mitigation

While the position is strong, risks remain. Management has proactive mitigation strategies for the most significant challenges.

1. Geopolitical Tensions & Export Controls

Risk: Revenue loss from restrictions, particularly regarding China (which represented ~29% of Q4 2024 net sales).

Mitigation: Active engagement with US, EU, and Dutch authorities; strategic geographic diversification of customer base (Taiwan, Korea, US, Europe).

2. Industry Cyclicality

Risk: Volatile revenue and cash flows due to semiconductor industry downturns. Customer order delays or cancellations.

Mitigation: Growing, stable service revenue (23% of total); long-term volume purchase agreements; strong balance sheet to weather downturns.

3. Supply Chain Concentration

Risk: Reliance on single-source suppliers for critical components, especially Carl Zeiss SMT for EUV optics, could halt production.

Mitigation: Strategic 24.9% equity stake in Carl Zeiss SMT; long-term framework agreements and co-investment in supplier capacity.

Investment Conclusion & Recommendation

ASML represents a rare combination of a monopolistic market position, excellent financial execution, and a reasonable valuation. It provides direct exposure to the most critical secular growth trends in technology.
